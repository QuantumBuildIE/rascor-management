## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 What are Toolbox Talks?

Toolbox Talks are short, focused safety training sessions that cover important health and safety topics relevant to your workplace. In construction and similar industries, these brief training sessions help keep safety at the forefront of everyone's mind.

In this system, Toolbox Talks are delivered digitally, allowing you to:
- Watch safety training videos
- Read educational content
- Take quizzes to test your understanding
- Sign off on completed training
- Keep a permanent record of your safety training

### 1.2 Why are They Important?

Toolbox Talks serve several critical purposes:

- **Safety Awareness**: Regular reminders about safe working practices help prevent accidents and injuries
- **Legal Compliance**: Many industries require documented safety training for all workers
- **Knowledge Retention**: Repeated exposure to safety information helps workers remember important procedures
- **Record Keeping**: Digital completion records provide proof of training for audits and inspections
- **Continuous Learning**: New hazards and best practices can be communicated quickly to all staff

### 1.3 Who Uses This System?

This system has two main types of users:

| User Type | What They Do |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Administrators** | Create and manage Toolbox Talks, assign them to employees, monitor completion progress, and generate reports |
| **Employees** | View their assigned Toolbox Talks, watch videos, read content, take quizzes, and sign off on completed training |

   - Check the box next to each employee you want to include
   - Use **"Select All"** or **"Clear"** buttons for quick selection

7. **Notes** (optional): Add any notes about this schedule

8. Click **"Create Schedule"** to confirm

## 4. Troubleshooting

### Video Won't Play

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Slow internet connection**
   - Wait a moment for the video to load
   - Try refreshing the page
   - Move to a location with better internet

2. **Browser compatibility**
   - Use a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
   - Update your browser to the latest version
   - Disable browser extensions that might block video

3. **Video format issue**
   - Contact your administrator - the video may need to be re-uploaded

> ðŸ’¡ **Tip:** If the video still won't play, try a different device or browser.

---

### Subtitles Not Showing

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Subtitles not available**
   - Not all videos have subtitles
   - Check if the CC button is visible on the video player

2. **Wrong language selected**
   - Click the subtitles button
   - Select a different language

3. **Subtitles still processing**
   - Subtitles may still be generating
   - Try again later or contact your administrator

---

### Can't Find Assigned Toolbox Talk

**Steps to troubleshoot:**

1. Check all tabs (Pending, In Progress, Overdue, Completed, All)
2. Make sure you're logged in with the correct account
3. The assignment may not have started yet - check with your administrator
4. Refresh the page to get the latest data

---

### Progress Not Saving

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Internet connection lost**
   - Check your internet connection
   - Wait for connection to restore and try again
   - Progress should resume automatically

2. **Session timed out**
   - Log in again
   - Return to the Toolbox Talk
   - Your previous progress should be saved

3. **Browser issue**
   - Try a different browser
   - Clear your browser cache

> ðŸ’¡ **Tip:** Your progress is automatically saved as you go. If you close the browser and return, you should be able to continue where you left off.

---

### Quiz Won't Submit

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Not all questions answered**
   - Scroll through all questions
   - Make sure every question has a selected answer

2. **Internet connection issue**
   - Check your connection
   - Try clicking "Submit" again

3. **Technical error**
   - Refresh the page
   - Your answers may need to be re-entered

---

### Contact Support

If you continue to experience problems:

1. Note the exact error message (if any)
2. Note what you were trying to do when the problem occurred
3. Contact your system administrator or IT support
4. Provide them with:
   - Your username
   - The Toolbox Talk title
   - Description of the problem
   - Any error messages you saw

## 5. Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Assignment** | A Toolbox Talk that has been assigned to a specific employee with a due date |
| **Completion Rate** | The percentage of assigned Toolbox Talks that have been completed |
| **Due Date** | The deadline by which an employee must complete their assigned Toolbox Talk |
| **Frequency** | How often a Toolbox Talk should be completed (Once, Weekly, Monthly, Annually) |
| **KPI** | Key Performance Indicator - a measurable value that shows how effectively something is being achieved |
| **Overdue** | An assignment that has passed its due date without being completed |
| **Pending** | An assignment that has been created but not yet started by the employee |
| **Quiz** | A set of questions to test understanding of the Toolbox Talk content |
| **Schedule** | A plan for when and to whom a Toolbox Talk will be assigned |
| **Section** | A part of the Toolbox Talk content that employees read through |
| **Signature** | Digital confirmation that an employee has completed and understood the training |
| **Toolbox Talk** | A short, focused safety training session covering a specific topic |
| **Transcript** | Written text of what is spoken in a video, used for subtitles |
| **Video Watch Percent** | The minimum percentage of the video an employee must watch |

## Quick Reference Cards

### For Employees: Completing a Toolbox Talk

1. Log in to RASCOR
2. Click "Toolbox Talks" in the menu
3. Find your assignment and click "Start" or "Continue"
4. Watch the video (if included)
5. Read all sections and mark as read
6. Take and pass the quiz (if required)
7. Sign to confirm completion
8. Done! Check the "Completed" tab to review

### For Administrators: Quick Scheduling

1. Go to Toolbox Talks > Talks
2. Click "Actions" on the talk you want to assign
3. Select "Schedule"
4. Set the date and frequency
5. Choose employees (or "Assign to all")
6. Click "Create Schedule"
7. Monitor progress on the Dashboard
